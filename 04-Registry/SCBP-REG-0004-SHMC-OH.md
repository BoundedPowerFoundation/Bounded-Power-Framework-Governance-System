The authority administers municipal governance for the City of Shaker Heights, Ohio. The authority enacts and enforces municipal ordinances, administers permitting and inspections, collects taxes and fees, operates municipal infrastructure and public services, contracts with vendors, allocates public resources, regulates land use and occupancy, coordinates emergency response, and operationalizes municipal policing and administrative enforcement functions through subordinate departments.

The authority may impose civil penalties, licensing restrictions, property-use limitations, administrative fees, service shutoffs, towing authorization, and code-enforcement actions within municipal jurisdictional boundaries. The authority also materially influences downstream decisions by courts, regional agencies, insurers, contractors, and residents through permitting, inspection, zoning, procurement, and public-safety classifications.
